Welcome

I am Aman Singh, Physics Graduate interested in fundamental theories from both theoratical and experimental point.

I am interested in fundamental questions in theoretical physics, particularly in high energy particle physics, quantum field theory, collider physics, and general relativity. My research interests focus on understanding the structure of fundamental interactions across different parametric scales, from quantum field theoretic descriptions to gravitational and cosmological regimes. I am especially interested in how effective descriptions of physical systems emerge in different limits and how principles such as the correspondence principle connect theories across scales. My work is motivated by exploring particle interactions in high-energy and high-density environments, using both analytical and computational approaches. More broadly, I am interested in how quantum field theory, gravitational physics, and collider phenomenology together contribute to a deeper understanding of fundamental physical laws.
