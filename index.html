My work employs both analytical and computational methods to explore fundamental interactions across diverse scales, ranging from quantum to cosmological regimes. Ultimately, I am driven by investigating how effective theories emerge in different limits and how these distinct fields intersect to deepen our understanding of fundamental physical laws.
